## 🎯 SEO OPTIMIZATION CHECKLIST

Use this checklist for EACH city page (Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Lahore, Peshawar):

### 1. CONTENT STRUCTURE & WRITING
- [ ] **Page Word Count**: 1200-1500 words minimum
- [ ] **H1 Tag**: Single, keyword-rich, descriptive (not generic)
- [ ] **H2 Tags**: 8-10 sections with proper hierarchy
- [ ] **H3 Tags**: Subsections under each H2
- [ ] **Paragraph Length**: Keep to 2-4 sentences max (mobile readability)
- [ ] **Bullet Points**: Use for lists over prose (easier scanning)
- [ ] **Unique Content**: Not copied from other city pages
- [ ] **Readability**: Simple words, short sentences, active voice

### 2. PRIMARY & LONG-TAIL KEYWORDS
**Primary**: `movers in [city]`, `packers and movers in [city]`

**Long-tail keywords to naturally include** (5-10 per page):
- `best movers in [city]`
- `professional packers [city]`
- `home shifting [city]`
- `office relocation [city]`
- `movers near [specific area]` (e.g., "movers near DHA Lahore")
- `[city] moving company`
- `affordable movers [city]`
- `experienced furniture movers [city]`
- `house shifting with packing [city]`
- `international moving from [city]`

**Implementation**: Spread across H2s, first paragraph, FAQ questions

### 3. META OPTIMIZATION
- [ ] **Meta Title** (50-60 chars):
  - Format: `Movers and Packers in [City] | Reliable [Service] & [Service]`
  - Example: `Movers and Packers in Lahore | Reliable Home Shifting & Office Relocation`
  
- [ ] **Meta Description** (150-160 chars):
  - Start with primary keyword
  - Include 2 specific service areas
  - Include number of years/clients or free offer
  - Include CTA (Call, Get Quote, Free Survey)
  - Example: `Professional movers and packers in Lahore serving DHA, Gulberg, Model Town. 15+ years, 5000+ clients. Free survey. Call 0300-9130211`

- [ ] **URL**: SEO-friendly (already set, e.g., `/movers-lahore`)
