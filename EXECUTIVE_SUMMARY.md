## ⚠️ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Don't duplicate content**: Each city page must be 100% unique
2. **Don't keyword stuff**: Integrate keywords naturally (8–10 per page, not 50)
3. **Don't ignore mobile**: Test on actual mobile devices, not just browsers
4. **Don't optimize only for robots**: Write for humans first, robots second
5. **Don't neglect local details**: Generic content ranks poorly (mention specific areas, landmarks)
6. **Don't forget internal linking**: It's critical for both UX and SEO
7. **Don't publish and ignore**: Monitor rankings weekly, adjust as needed
8. **Don't compete with big names only**: Target long-tail keywords first (easier wins)
