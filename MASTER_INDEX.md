## ⚠️ COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID

1. **Duplicate Content Across Cities**
   - ❌ Copy-pasting MoversLahore content into MoversPeshawar
   - ✅ Write unique content for each city with local examples

2. **Keyword Stuffing**
   - ❌ "movers movers movers in lahore moving moving"
   - ✅ 8-10 long-tail keywords naturally integrated

3. **Ignoring Mobile**
   - ❌ Page looks good on desktop but breaks on mobile
   - ✅ Test on actual mobile device, not just browser

4. **Neglecting Internal Linking**
   - ❌ Each city page exists in isolation
   - ✅ Link cities to each other (users find related services)

5. **Publishing Without Testing**
   - ❌ Typos, broken links, formatting issues in live page
   - ✅ Full QA checklist before production deployment

6. **Forgetting to Monitor (Set and Forget)**
   - ❌ Optimize pages, then never check performance
   - ✅ Weekly ranking checks, monthly reporting

7. **Not Differentiating Cities**
   - ❌ "This works for Lahore so copy it everywhere"
   - ✅ Customize for city-specific challenges, areas, demographics

8. **Expecting Overnight Results**
   - ❌ Expecting rank 1 in week 2
   - ✅ Realistic timeline: Positions 15-25 → 3-5 in 3-4 months
